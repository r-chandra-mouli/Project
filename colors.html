About Us

Colours is an earnest endeavour in the mental health domain. The club aims at breaking the stigma around mental health conversations and providing awareness regarding various mental health issues. Let's unbox the colours of our life!

Information

Children's mental health is a pressing issue that requires focused attention and proactive measures from parents and society alike to ensure their overall well-being. Children are highly susceptible to mental health issues due to a range of reasons, including genetic predispositions, environmental factors, and experiences such as trauma or abuse. Therefore, it's crucial to stay mindful of the signs and symptoms of mental health problems in children and take appropriate steps to address them.
