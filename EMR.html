Welcome To Embedded System and Robotics

A club dedicated to building knowledge of embedded system design, robotics, image processing and machine learning in NIT Kurukshetra and beyond

About Us

It gives us immense pleasure to successfully conclude the #workshop on "Labyrinth Solver Car". The workshop started with the #fundamentals of embedded systems including various sensors, motors, followed by theoretical and hands-on knowledge on micro-controllers like #arduino and #nodeMCU. Every team made two bots during the course of this workshop - a line follower car and a mobile controlled wireless RC car. It was an amazing experience to guide some of the greatest minds of NIT Kurukshetra. Everyone came with a great zeal to learn and were heartily satisfied with the overall flow of the workshop.
