About Us

LSD (Let's Start Dance) Dragons is a dance group founded in 2014 and funded by an institute. We are a passionate crew of dancers, and our aim is to spread the joy of dance. We have achieved remarkable milestones throughout the years, showcasing our talent and dedication:

Achievements

  • Multiple winners at Confluence in 2014, 2016, 2017, and 2022
  • Participated in INSOMNIA 2022
  • Runner-up in 2018 and 2019
  • Performed at IISER Mohali in 2015, 2016, and 2022
  • Entertained the audience at MMU Mullana in 2018

Information

At LSD Dancers, we celebrate various cultural events, including Holi, where our crew members showcase their talent and enthusiasm by performing to traditional songs. Additionally, we frequently participate in freshers' events, allowing us to share our energy and passion with the audience.
