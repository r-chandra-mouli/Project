Welcome To Shiksha

Empowering underprivileged children through education

About Us

'Shiksha' under the aegis of Students' Club for Social Action (SCSA), takes upon itself the responsibility of enlightening the lives of children by educating them. If you feed a man a fish, you feed him for a day. If you teach a man to fish, you feed him for a lifetime. Despite the unfortunate reality, the vicinity of NIT KKR is home to a significant number of children deprived of basic education and lacking the means to obtain it. At Shiksha, we strive to empower these underprivileged yet determined children to attain the fundamental skills necessary for a prosperous life, paving the way for a brighter and more promising future.

Information

1. Free-of-cost education is provided to underprivileged students (Class 5th to 12th).
2. A free-of-cost scholarship exam is conducted to motivate the students, and the one who tops the examination receives awards like notebooks, pens, money, etc.
3. Sports activities are arranged for students (such as Judo).
4. A Science Exhibition is conducted every year, allowing young children to showcase their talents in the field of science.
5. Every year, an event called "Make a Wish" is organized where all the wishes of children are fulfilled by the Shiksha club.
